Case Study

Xd Plugins

The Opportunity

Seeing gaps in the Xd plugin ecosystem, our team built modular plugins accelerating design and expanding what could be done natively in Xd. For one of them, we partnered with Adobe Commerce to build a faster way to design and customize ecommerce pages.

What I Did

Designed both Xd plugin experiences end-to-end, including select templates. For Adobe Commerce, I designed a PWA design system that is easily customizable, enabling SMB merchants to build and adapt their storefronts directly within Xd.
