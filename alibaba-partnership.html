Case Study

Alibaba Strategic Partnership

The Opportunity

Alibaba came to Adobe to better meet the needs of their creative and merchant communities — spanning Photoshop and XD API integrations, video editing, Adobe Stock, and emerging AR/3D capabilities. The opportunity was to bring the full breadth of Creative Cloud into one of the world's largest commerce and creator platforms.

What I Did

Served as Chinese-language liaison between Alibaba and Adobe's Strategic Partnerships team, CC app leads, and VP Alexandru Costin — identifying integration opportunities for Creative Cloud into a platform used by millions of designers and SMBs across China. Designed all integration mockups, including XD and Photoshop plugin concepts.
