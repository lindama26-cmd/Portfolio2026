The Problem

K-12 educators needed Express to be easier to use with their students before they'd adopt it. Bringing the assignment workflow into Express makes this construct the foundation for classroom workflows — class creation, assignments, progress tracking, galleries, and group projects — and it's built to scale into any Adobe app.

What I Did

Starting from a research paper by Tara Kola, I designed the Classrooms and Assignments construct, co-wrote the PRD with my PM, and built it with ACPC. Galleries — another design-led idea — became the most beloved feature and the biggest growth flywheel for Express for Education.
