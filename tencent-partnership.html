Case Study

Tencent Strategic Partnership

The Opportunity

Tencent, the world's largest social and gaming platform, approached Adobe to unlock deeper document workflows for its vast ecosystem — spanning WeChat, WeCom, and QQ. The integration opportunity centered on bringing Acrobat tools as a first-party plugin into Tencent's product environment, reaching hundreds of millions of users across China.

What I Did

Bridging Chinese and Western product environments, I served as primary design and liaison lead between Tencent and Adobe — translating cultural and technical requirements across both organizations. Defined integration UX frameworks for the Acrobat plugin and designed all partnership proposal mockups and executive-facing materials.
